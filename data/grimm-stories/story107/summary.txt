A rich sister refuses to share bread with her poor sister and her poor sister's five children. The rich sister's husband cuts himself a slice of bread and blood flows from the loaf. The rich sister is terrified and tells her husband what happened. The husband goes to help the poor sister. He finds her praying. Three of the children are already dead. She says God will now take care of them. As she says this, the two youngest children die along with their mother.
