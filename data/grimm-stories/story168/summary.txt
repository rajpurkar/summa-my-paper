The daughter of a miller is promised in marriage to a suitor. The daughter is afraid of her betrothed, but she visits his house. She learns he is actually a killer. When the maiden returns home, she tells her father what she learned at the suitor's house. The wedding is held as planned and as all the guests sit at the table, they are asked to tell a story. The bride tells the story of what happened to her at the murderer's house. Realizing he has been found out, the murderer tries to leave, but the guests apprehend him.
