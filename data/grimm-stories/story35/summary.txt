A stepmother is cruel to her stepdaughter and makes her go out to pick strawberries in the middle of winter. The stepdaughter meets three men who bless her and help her. The girl's stepsister is jealous and wants to be blessed also. She visits the men but they curse her instead of blessing her. The stepdaughter marries a king. The jealous stepsister and stepmother arrange to have the new queen killed. The king learns of the scheme and brings the queen back to life. He has the stepsister and stepmother killed.
