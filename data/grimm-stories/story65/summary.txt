A girl is distracted by a wolf on her way to her grandmother's house. He learns her plans and goes to the grandmother's house where he eats the grandmother. When the girl arrives at the house, the wolf eats her also. A man passing by saves the grandmother and her granddaughter from the wolf's belly. The next time a wolf tries to distract the girl, she will not listen to him. She instead goes to her grandmother's house where she and her grandmother trick the wolf and the wolf is killed.
