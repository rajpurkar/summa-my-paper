As a result of injury, a solider is dismissed from the king's service with no pay and no way to support himself. He meets up with a witch. The witch allows him to do work for her. The witch asks the soldier to go down into a well to retrieve a blue light. The soldier does so but refuses to give it to her until she pulls him fully out of the well. This angers this witch. She drops him into the well and leaves him. The soldier assumes he will die in the well and finds his pipe. The man lights the pipe from the blue light, and a dwarf who must grant all of the soldier's wishes appears.
