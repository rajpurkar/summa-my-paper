A clerk who finds him self at an art event, where he happens to be seated behind a VIP. shortly afterwards, the clerk sneezes and spatters all over the VIP's bald head. his life changes from that point on. when he becomes totally occupied with an obsessive idea to show his apology and respect to the VIP. he frequently tries to apologize. following the VIP from his office to his home. when he gets no attention from the VIP, he becomes totally depressed and starts writing him a letter of his sincere apology. this letter consumes him all night and by the end he collapses and dies on his desk while writing his last line.
